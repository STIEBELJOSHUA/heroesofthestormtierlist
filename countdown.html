Countdown to the end of (UNIX) time

A countdown to 3:14:07 on Tuesday, 19 January 2038, when unix time will cease to function on 32 bit systems. This is because unix time is the number of seconds since the unix epoch (1 January 1970), and in early 2038, the number of seconds since the epoch will reach 2,147,483,647 or 1111111111111111111111111111111 in binary. See the problem?
